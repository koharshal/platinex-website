For high-volume Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs) in sectors ranging from automotive and aerospace to medical devices and consumer electronics, surface treatment is often the final, most critical bottleneck in the supply chain.

When you require millions of components plated to exact micron tolerances, relying on a general job-shop plater introduces significant risks: fluctuating lead times, inconsistent quality control due to shared chemical baths, and a lack of process customization.

At **Platinex Industries**, we recognize that true scale requires a different approach. Beyond our standard, high-capacity electroplating services, we offer a specialized partnership model designed for industry leaders: **the design, construction, and operation of dedicated, custom-built surface treatment lines and entire plants.**

## The Limitations of the "Job Shop" Model

A traditional electroplating facility operates as a job shop. Today, they might be running commercial zinc plating for hardware; tomorrow, high-spec nickel for switchgear.

While this model offers flexibility, it presents challenges for OEMs with highly specific, non-negotiable requirements:

- **Chemistry Compromises:** Shared baths often run on "average" parameters that work adequately for most parts, but aren't perfectly optimized for yours.
- **Capacity Constraints:** Your urgent, high-volume orders are forced to compete for line time with dozens of other clients.
- **Contamination Risks:** Different base metals and pretreatment requirements running on the same line increase the risk of cross-contamination in the baths.

## The Dedicated Line Solution

When an OEM partners with Platinex to establish a dedicated process line—or an entirely separate, captive electroplating plant—those limitations vanish. We essentially become an extension of your own manufacturing floor, located right here in Nashik, Maharashtra.

### 1. Tailored Chemistry and Process Engineering

A dedicated line is engineered from the ground up for _your_ specific component.

- **Custom Rectifiers and Pulse Plating:** We can install specialized power supplies optimized for your part's geometry to eliminate the "dog-bone" thickness effect.
- **Optimized Bath Formulations:** We don't use generic chemistry. We formulate the exact brighteners, carriers, and sealers required to maximize your component's salt-spray resistance or solderability.
- **Custom Racking/Barreling:** We design bespoke jigs and fixtures that maximize throughput while minimizing contact marks and ensuring perfect current distribution.

### 2. Guaranteed Capacity and Lead Times

With a dedicated line, your production schedule dictates our plating schedule. There is no waiting in the queue. This translates to Just-In-Time (JIT) delivery, drastically reducing your inventory holding costs and ensuring your assembly lines never stop.

### 3. Integrated Quality Control

When running a single component continuously, we can implement statistical process control (SPC) at a level impossible in a job shop. We establish automated dosing systems that keep chemical concentrations mathematically perfect 24/7, and integrate specific, non-destructive testing (like continuous XRF thickness measurement) tailored to your exact drawing tolerances.
